How it Works


We're all in this together.

so let's be safer

together.

Kehlp is a review platform which allows members from the community to write about their experiences going to businesses in-person. It provides store information during these troubling times where keeping strict hygenic standards is more important than ever. Real people post real reviews on Kehlp. This very useful especially for those at a higher risk of catching COVID-19, including immunocompromised, the elderly and children.

Follow the guidelines below so that your in-store experience can help someone else safe.


  1. Visit a business

    2. Go shopping, get a haircut or eat at a restaurant but make sure do so responsibly!

    Follow the CDC and your local government's guidelines for going out during COVID-19


  2. Keep an eye out during your visit

    3. Are customers wearing masks and social distancing?

    Are store employees routinely sanitizing frequently used items and surfaces?

    Take notice of the business' new hygenic standards and what changes they have implemented to protect customers.

  3. Come back onto Kehlp after returning home

    4. Go to the submit page and write a meaningful and truthful review about your visit

    Let others know about the store's condition so they know what to look out for.

    Remember to include all the important information!
